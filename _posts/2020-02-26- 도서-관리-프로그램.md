### 텍스트 창

도서 검색을 위한

```java
		//프레임
		GridBagConstraints gbc_textField = new GridBagConstraints();
		gbc_textField.fill = GridBagConstraints.HORIZONTAL;
		gbc_textField.gridx = 1;
		gbc_textField.gridy = 1;
		contentPane.add(textField, gbc_textField);
```
